**Tag** — mono uppercase chip for tech-stack and category labels.

```jsx
<Tag>WordPress</Tag>
<Tag variant="accent" dot>LIVE</Tag>
<Tag variant="solid">WooCommerce</Tag>
```

Variants: `default` (hairline outline), `solid` (inset fill), `accent` (neon wash). `dot` adds a
leading status dot. Always JetBrains Mono, uppercase, pill.
